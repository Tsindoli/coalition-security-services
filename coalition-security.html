Who Are We?

Coalition Security Services started as a small firm in 1980. We security services such as perimeter patrol, parking security, prevention of unauthorized activity or entry, traffic regulation, access control, fire and theft prevention and detection. We protect personnel and homes, offices, factories, sports clubs, construction sites, hotels and other assets. We also provide Cash-in-Transit, Canine and Body Guard Services.

Our Mission is to provide a high standard of security that satisfies the needs and aspirations of our Clients. We employ over 2000 guards working in about 75 institutions. We have about 120 patrol vehicles and 5 armoured vehicles.

Our primary objective is to prevent crime against persons, property and proprietary information. We maintain confidentiality, integrity, availabilty and accountability.
